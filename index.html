About Me

I am Mehrdad, a product designer from Ottawa.

My journey has been anything but traditional—driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and a relentless pursuit of efficiency. My career started in procurement and supply chain management, where I spent over a decade optimizing workflows, negotiating contracts, and ensuring seamless operations in public transportation. But something was missing: the human connection in how people experience products and services.

That realization led me to web design and programming, where I first developed a deep understanding of front-end development, responsive design, and system management. As I worked on digital interfaces, I became increasingly fascinated by how users interact with technology, which naturally led me to UX/UI design. I immersed myself in user research, interaction design, and high-fidelity prototyping, refining my ability to create seamless, engaging experiences. Wanting to push the boundaries further, I explored 3D visualization and interactive design using Unity, expanding my skill set to include immersive and dynamic digital environments.

What sets me apart is my systems-thinking approach—I see products not just as screens but as experiences that must be functional, seamless, and business-aligned. My background in procurement and contract management gives me a unique lens on efficiency and decision-making, allowing me to bridge the gap between business strategy and user needs.

Now, as a UX designer, I bring precision, reliability, and a deep sense of ownership to every project. I don’t just design interfaces—I craft thoughtful experiences that are data-driven, user-focused, and built for impact.

I’m always up for a challenge, whether it’s optimizing a digital product, leading a design strategy, or figuring out the perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality. Let’s create something meaningful together.
